Week 1 — Audit

  • Map agent workflow, tools, permissions, customer-visible failure modes.
  • Define scoped invariants: what must be true before release.
  • Build a failure corpus from logs, demos, transcripts, tickets, and edge cases.
  • Design the release-gate rubric: pass/fail/needs-human-review.

Week 2 — Gate

  • Turn the rubric into executable checks and regression suites.
  • Wire gates into CI or the team's existing deploy/release process.
  • Add observability hooks and a lightweight risk register.
  • Run a before/after release-readiness demo on the target workflow.

Training — Keep it

  • Two 90-minute workshops for engineers and product leads.
  • A written method: how to add cases, update rubrics, and avoid eval theater.
  • Hand-off docs: gate owner, escalation path, release checklist, next 30 days.
